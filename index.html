Delete Account & Data Policy

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from The Mafia, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion?

To request account and data deletion, please email us at: contact@aapnbazaar.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Please include the phone number or username associated with your account so we can identify and process your request.

What Data is Deleted?

  • Your profile information (name, username, avatar)
  • Your friend list and chat history
  • Game history and statistics
  • Any uploaded images or media
  • Analytics and log data associated with your account

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at contact@aapnbazaar.com.
