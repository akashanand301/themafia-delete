Delete Account & Data Policy

The Mafia does not require users to create an account. However, if you wish to delete any data associated with your usage, follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion?

To request data deletion, please email us at: contact@aapnbazaar.com with the subject **"Data Deletion Request"**.

What Data is Deleted?

  • We do not store personal accounts.
  • Any analytics/log data is deleted after a retention period.
  • For manual data deletion, please contact support.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at contact@aapnbazaar.com.
